Typography

Font

The Design system states:

If your service is publicly available on a subdomain other than service.gov.uk, use an alternative typeface like Helvetica or Arial.

In PBI I use Segoe UI as it has increased line height and is easier to read than Arial.

Paragraphs

Body

The default paragraph font size should be set to 12px.

Lead paragraph

A lead paragraph is an introductory paragraph that you can use at the top of a page to summarise the content. Lead paragraphs use 14px type and should only be used once per page if needed.

Body small

Smaller paragraph text is 10.5px. Used for minor details, use sparingly.

The majority of your body copy should use the standard 12px body paragraph size.
