Categorical

Used to colour multiple individual data points, or entire graphs, and make them distinguishable. Applied in sequence to create contrast between adjoining colours which aid with vision deficiencies.

Although eight colours are provided a limit of four categories is advised to prevent a chart becoming too cluttered. Please consider your data representation carefully.

Overarching

These exist as a palette to select individual colours to standardise text, backgrounds, etc. It is made up of the text, label, axis, Categorical, and prominent Sequential colours.

Care must be taken to use these colours in line with the guidelines, e.g. the yellow focus colour should not be used outside of highlighting a focussed element.

Discrepancies in usage will dilute the representation of the meanings associated with the colours.
